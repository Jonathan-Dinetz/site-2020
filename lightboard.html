Project Info
An idea formulated while on a backpacking trip deep in the high sierra, the Lightboard was devised as a psychological aid for me during a long isolated period I spent at home during the pandemic. It is a grid of 100 LED lightbulbs that can be controlled through this website. The lights form a small canvas for friends (or anyone on the planet really) to write a message, draw a smiley face, or just annoy me by turning on the whole grid at 4am. The warmth of the lights makes for a particularly uplifting experience when some unnamed actor suddenly brings the board to life. The project was made possible by the immense contributions of my buddy Andrew of the Colorado College computer science department.
