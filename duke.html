Project Info
The delivery package and supporting materials for an Oscar nominated film, The Duke of Burgundy. The film focuses on two women locked in a straining S&M relationship while studying lepidopterology at a summer insitute. Combining imagery from butterfly wings and sex toys, the package aims to deliver the fantastical, nearly science-fiction auroa the film evokes. Each of the materials within the box uses elements of sound, tacility, or scent to relate the skin-crawling beauty the Duke of Burgendy contains. This project is still being documented and the project page will be expanded in the coming weeks.
